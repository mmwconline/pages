Welcome to MMWC

Our mission is to empower Muslim women by providing them with the skills and training necessary for self advocacy, leadership and economic security. To advocate for Islam and Muslims by promoting an accurate understanding of the faith, to encourage dialogue amongst all people, to protect civil liberties and to build relationships and coalitions that work for justice, harmony, peace and understanding.

We believe ...

  • In upholding the dignity of every human being
  • Diversity in race and ideology is a source of strength for our Country
  • Civil rights of all Americans must be protected regardless of faith
  • Building relations and establishing dialogue with individuals and institutions that advocate for justice and human rights
  • Most prejudice and hatred is a result of ignorance

Our Programs

Join us at any of our events and be a part of MMWC

"So How's It Going?" Dinner

Monthly, 3rd Wednesday @ 6:30pm

Join the MMWC for a potluck event that presents an opportunity for Muslim reverts to have an open discussion.

Networking Brunch

Monthly, last Friday @ 10am

Beginner Arabic

Weekly, Sunday @ 10am

During this 13 week program, students work together in an engaging environment so they are able to communicate effectively in Arabic.

Intermediate Arabic

Women's Tafsir

Milwaukee Muslim Film Festival

MMWC is the founder of the Milwaukee Muslim Film Festival, one of only a few city-wide Muslim Film Festivals in the country.

The Milwaukee Muslim Film Festival is committed to discovering and sharing some of the most exciting and thought provoking films from around the world. Their aim is to use film as a means for presenting topics and issues that are timely, relevant and generate meaningful discussion about Muslims and the Muslim world.

Their dedicated team carefully selects films from across the globe that transcend countries, languages and cultures while highlighting the common humanity among people of all races, ethnicities and religious beliefs.
